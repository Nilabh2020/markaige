Security & trust

Your data stays yours.

Enterprise-grade isolation, deployment on your terms, and a simple promise we never break: your data never trains another customer's model, and the weights we build are yours to keep.

Data isolation

Your data is fully isolated per engagement. It is never pooled, never used to train other customers' models, and never leaves your agreed boundary.

Encryption everywhere

Encrypted in transit and at rest, with managed keys — or bring your own. Secrets and credentials are scoped, rotated and audited.

Ownership & exit

On Customize and Foundry the weights are yours. Export the model, host it elsewhere, walk away — no lock-in, ever.

Access control

Role-based access, SSO/SAML, least-privilege by default and full audit logs across the data and model lifecycle.

Privacy by design

PII handling, de-identification and data-retention controls are built into the refiner engine, not bolted on after.

Monitored in production

ModelOps watches for drift, abuse and regressions under SLA, with alerting and a clear incident-response path.
