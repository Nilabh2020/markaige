How each tier works

The further you go, the more of the stack we build for you.

01
Integrate
Live in weeks

The fastest path to production AI. You bring a product and a knowledge base; we stand up one of our pre-built Markaige models around it. We wire in advanced RAG so answers are grounded in your data, connect your tool stack over MCP, and host the whole thing on our infrastructure with monitoring and a standard SLA.

Best for teams who need a capable, grounded assistant or agent shipped quickly — without training a model of their own yet. You can graduate to Customize whenever you're ready.

02
Customize
Your own fleet

Now the model learns your business. Our data cleaner & refiner engine turns your raw, messy data into a golden dataset, and we fine-tune specialized models on it — reasoning, coding, your domain — then combine them into one system. Every model is benchmarked against the metrics that actually matter to you, not a generic leaderboard.

Continuous training and full ModelOps keep it sharp in production under a committed SLA. Best for teams who want AI that genuinely understands their domain and stays current as the business changes.

03
Foundry
Built from scratch

No starting point but yours. In Foundry we build everything from scratch — even the base model. We design a bespoke architecture and parameter set for your use case, train it on dedicated compute against your golden dataset, and harden it through the full benchmark and ModelOps lifecycle.

You own the result outright — your data, your weights, your model — hosted privately or on-prem with an embedded Markaige team. Best for organizations where the model is the product, or where regulation, IP and frontier performance demand a system no one else has.

Common questions

Can we move up a tier later?
Always. Most teams start on Integrate to ship fast, then graduate to Customize as they gather data — and to Foundry when the model becomes core IP. Your work carries forward at every step.
Who owns the model and the data?
You do. Your data stays yours on every plan. On Customize and Foundry, the fine-tuned and from-scratch weights are yours to keep and host wherever you choose.
Is the pricing fixed?
The figures shown are indicative starting points. Because every engagement is a services partnership, final pricing is scoped to your data volume, model complexity, hosting and SLA needs.
How long does Foundry take?
Building a base model from scratch is a longer commitment than fine-tuning. We scope a roadmap up front — dataset, architecture, training and benchmarking milestones — so you always know where the model stands.

Not sure which plan fits?

Tell us what your product needs to do. We'll recommend the right tier and scope it with you — no pressure, no lock-in.
