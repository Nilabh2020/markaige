How we work

A partnership, not a hand-off.

Building a model is an engagement, not a download. Here's exactly how a Markaige build runs — what we own, what we need from you, and how long each stage takes.

01
Week 1–2

Discover

We map the use case, success metrics and constraints with your team, then scope the right tier and a delivery roadmap. You leave with a clear plan, timeline and definition of done.

We own: scoping & architectureYou bring: goals, stakeholders, access
02
Week 2–5

Data

Our cleaner and refiner engine turns your raw, messy sources into a golden dataset — deduplicated, labelled and high-signal. This stage moves the needle on final quality more than any other.

We own: cleaning, refining, curationYou bring: raw data & domain experts
03
Week 4–10+

Build

We fine-tune — or, in Foundry, train from scratch — the specialist models your use case needs, then combine them into one system. Architecture and parameters are tuned to your domain, not a generic default.

We own: training & orchestrationYou bring: feedback in review loops
04
Continuous

Benchmark

Every model is measured against an evaluation set built from your real-world tasks — not a public leaderboard. You see the numbers, the failure cases, and exactly where it stands before anything ships.

We own: eval harness & reportingYou bring: acceptance criteria
05
Go-live

Deploy

We ship to production on our hosting, your VPC, or fully on-prem — wired into your tool stack over MCP. One endpoint for your product; the fleet runs behind it. Your weights stay yours.

We own: deployment & integrationYou bring: environment & sign-off
06
Ongoing SLA

Operate

Full ModelOps: monitoring, retraining and continuous improvement so the model never goes stale. We maintain and run it under a committed SLA while your business — and its data — keeps changing.

We own: monitoring, retraining, SLAYou bring: new data & priorities
